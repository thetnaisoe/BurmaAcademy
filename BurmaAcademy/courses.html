Available Courses

We are offering the following classes based on the proficiency of the language. Check these out to know which would suit the best for you.

Beginner

Embark on your journey to learn the beautiful Burmese language with our Beginner Class at Burma Academy. Designed for those with little to no prior knowledge of Burmese, this course provides a solid foundation in essential vocabulary, grammar, and conversational skills. Our experienced instructors will guide you step by step, making the learning process engaging and enjoyable. By the end of the Beginner Class, you'll have a strong grasp of basic Burmese language elements and be ready to communicate confidently in everyday situations.

Intermediate

Take your Burmese language skills to the next level with our Intermediate Class at Burma Academy. Designed for learners with a basic understanding of Burmese, this course focuses on expanding your vocabulary, enhancing your grammar knowledge, and developing your ability to engage in more complex conversations. Through interactive lessons and immersive exercises, our expert instructors will help you build fluency and confidence in expressing yourself effectively in various real-life contexts. Join the Intermediate Class and unlock new opportunities for meaningful communication in Burmese.

Advanced

For those seeking mastery of the Burmese language, our Advanced Class at Burma Academy offers a challenging and enriching learning experience. Tailored for proficient speakers, this course delves deeper into advanced grammar structures, idiomatic expressions, and nuanced language usage. Through stimulating discussions, reading authentic materials, and refining your writing skills, you'll develop a sophisticated command of Burmese. Our dedicated instructors will provide personalized guidance and foster a dynamic learning environment to help you reach a high level of fluency and cultural understanding.
